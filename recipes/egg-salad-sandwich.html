Best Darn Egg Salad Sandwich Recipe

Follow these simple instructions to make the best darn egg salad sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 tbs Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbs Scallions (Green Onions)
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 2 Sliced Bread

Instructions

  1. Boil water in pot.
  2. Once water starts boiling, add eggs and leave for 8 minutes.
  3. After 8 minutes, rinse hard-boiled egg in cold water for 2 minutes.
  4. Peel the egg shells off.
  5. Separate the yolk and egg whites.
  6. Slice the egg whites into small pieces. Mash yolk with fork.
  7. Add scallions, mayo, egg yolk and whites, and honey together and mix.
  8. Toast your choice of sliced bread.
  9. Add egg salad onto your toasted bread.
  10. Serve and chow-down!
