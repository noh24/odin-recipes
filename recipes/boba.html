How To Make Boba Tea

Follow these instructions to make 6 servings of boba tea at home.

Ingredients

  • Boba Powder Boba Tea Powder on Amazon
  • Tapioca Pearls Best Tapioca Pearls on Amazon
  • Water
  • Black Tea
  • Brown Sugar

Equipment

  • Gas/Electric Stove Tops
  • Pot to boil water
  • Strainer
  • Laddle
  • Cup for boba tea

Instructions

Preparing Tapioca Pearls

  1. Fill pot with 2L of clean water.
  2. Boil water in pot.
  3. Add 12oz of Tapioca Pearls into the pot with boiling water.
  4. Continue boil the pearls for 20 minutes, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour pearls into strainer.
  6. Rinse with cold water for 1 minute.

Preparing Boba Tea (6 serving)

  1. Add 1L of hot tap water into pot.
  2. Add 8 oz of brown sugar into pot.
  3. Add 8oz of boba tea powder into pot.
  4. Add 300mL of black tea.
  5. Stir until powder and brown sugar dissolves.
  6. Add tapioca pearls and boba tea into cup.
  7. Drink the boba tea in the cup.
