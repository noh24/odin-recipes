Flourless Protein Pancake Recipe

Follow these instructions to make flourless protein pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder. This recipe is suitable for flavored whey or pea protein powder.
  • Eggs provides structure, leavening, flabour and richness.
  • Banana will be used as a natural sweetener.
  • Baking Powder provides more leavening (more fluffier).
  • Butter, cooking oil/spray

Preparation

  • 3tbs of protein powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp of baking powder

Method

  1. Blend or whisk ingredients together. Mixture should be a thick, pourable batter; that's not too runny or stiff.
  2. Heat a fry-pan over a medium to low heat. Grease well with oil or butter - prevents stick.
  3. Pour pancake batter into pan.
  4. Leave to cook until golden brown color and edges are slightly lifted up.
  5. Flip when one-side is ready. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Serve and chow-down!
